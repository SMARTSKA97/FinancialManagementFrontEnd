Our Mission

Financial Planner was born from the frustration of managing money across multiple accounts, apps, and spreadsheets. We wanted a single, beautiful, secure platform that makes personal finance management feel effortless — not like a chore.

Our team believes that financial awareness is the first step to financial freedom. That's why we've built tools that give you real-time visibility into your spending, income, and net worth — all wrapped in a premium experience.
